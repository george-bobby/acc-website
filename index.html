    About Us

    The Association of Christian Christies (ACC) is a thriving community at Christ (Deemed to Be University), uniting over 5,000 Christian students. We aim to spread the good news, fostering fellowship and a positive campus environment. By organizing events like Leadership Training programs, Discipleship Training Programme, Bible Fest, Retreats, Outreach initiatives and celebrate Holy Mass, Christmas and we strive to help students lead fulfilling Christian lives and make a positive impact. Additionally, ACC offers various domains like Animation, Audio Visual, Media & Documentation, Liturgy, and Outreach, allowing members to glorify God through their talents.
