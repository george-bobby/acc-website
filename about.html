    About Us

    Know us

    Association of Christian Christies is a community of all Christian students studying in Christ (Deemed To Be University). We , spread the message of Jesus and help ourselves lead a good Christian life and strive to make a change through our activities.

    ACC creates fellowship and bonding between fellow Christites and our vision is to create a positive atmosphere on campus and glorify God. We organize Holy Mass,Leadership Training Programmes, Retreats, Fests, Feast Celebrations, conduct intercessionary prayers,Outreach programmes etc.ACC community has 4500+ members spanning across the 5 campuses of Christ (Deemed To Be University).

    ACC has its domains through which the members can exhibit their talents and glorify God.The domains are: Animation Team, Audio Visual Team, Media & Documentation Team, Liturgy Team, Outreach Team and each domain have it's own volunteer body.

    Every day, the members of the ACC meet at St. Kuriakose Chapel for an intercession wherein they pray together for the university, the students, the world and for any special intentions. Also on every First Friday, a holy mass is organised in the Chapel by students from various departments in the University. We share an amazing fellowship with God.
